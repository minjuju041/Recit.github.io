In an era of instant uploads and disappearing stories, our deepest personal memories are often reduced to a pixelated blur. We capture everything, yet feel nothing.

Récit kit was born from a desire to reclaim the weight of the moment. Not to share, but to keep. Not to inhabit a screen, but to inhabit a space.
